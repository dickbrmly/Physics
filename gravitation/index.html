A day's travel occurs every half second and horizontal force is adjusted to equal vertical force due to uneven distances.

Both bodies are under gravitational force toward one another. The planet has the mass equal to earth and the moon is equal to our moon.

The velocity in meters-per-second is close to our moon's velocity. Both mass and velocity are adjustable for both bodies. Reloading the page will reset the values.

The Run / Stop button will stop the objects in position.

Another interesting condition is setting the moon to the earth's mass and giving a velocity of (0,750) and the planet a velocity of (0,-750).
